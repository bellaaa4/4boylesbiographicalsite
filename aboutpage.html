How I got into design

In 7th and 8th grade, I took a class with the “tech” teacher that included coding and film. This got me interested in taking computer-related classes since I found working with computers something I really enjoyed. I was offered to take this class since it combined things I was passionate about: art and computer design. When I was presented with this opportunity, of course I had to take it.

My Interests / Hobbies

I'm super passionate about design and all sorts of things including art. I've also played a multitude of sports throughout my high school years including soccer and basketball! I'm really passionate about all sorts of entertainment media — I'm a huge movie fanatic. My favorites include "All the Bright Places" (a real tear jerker) and "Waves".
